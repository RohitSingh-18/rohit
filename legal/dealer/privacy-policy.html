Privacy Policy for Dealerships

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Gaddiyaan we values the trust of the dealerships we work with. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide as a registered dealership on our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Dealership Information: Business name, contact details, dealership address, and registration details.
  • Vehicle Listings: Information about vehicles provided for marketing purposes, including make, model, price, and condition.
  • Financial Data: Payment details related to registration and lead generation payments.
  • Usage Data: Data on how you interact with our platform, including login details and activity logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To list your vehicles and market them to potential customers.
  • To share your contact details with interested customers for direct communication.
  • For internal business purposes, such as billing and customer support.
  • To ensure compliance with applicable laws and enforce our terms.

3. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only as necessary:

  • With Customers: Vehicle details and dealership contact information are shared with customers who inquire about your listings.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or regulatory authorities.

4. Your Responsibilities

  • You must ensure that all vehicle information provided is accurate, complete, and up to date.
  • You are responsible for addressing any customer concerns or disputes related to your vehicles.

5. Security of Your Data

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, as with any online platform, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of our platform indicates acceptance of any changes.
